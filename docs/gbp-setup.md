# Google Business Profile (GBP) Setup Guide

To maximize local and national visibility, it is highly recommended to set up and optimize a Google Business Profile for STRONGERbuilt.

## Steps to Claim and Optimize Your Profile

1. **Claim the Profile**
   - Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/) and sign in with your business Google account.
   - Search for "STRONGERbuilt LLC" or click "Add your business to Google".
   - Follow the prompts to claim or create the profile.

2. **Set the Primary Category**
   - Select **Business management consultant** or **Government relations consultant** as your primary category.

3. **Add Business Details**
   - **Phone Number:** Add your business phone: `(408) 657-6116`.
   - **Website:** Link to your primary domain: `https://www.strongerbuilt.us`.

4. **Solicit Client Reviews**
   - Reach out to past successful clients and ask them to leave an honest review on your new profile. Aim for 5–10 real client reviews to establish initial credibility. 
   - *Note: Do not create fake reviews or incentivize reviews against Google's policies.*

5. **Regular Updates**
   - Post monthly "bid-tip" updates or share recent successful contract awards (without violating client confidentiality). This signals to Google that your business is active and authoritative in the government contracting space.
